About Me

I'm KRDN1337 — a self-taught hacker and programmer obsessed with how software works at the lowest level. This site documents my journey learning Reverse Engineering and C++ from zero, in public, with zero filter.

Every tutorial I publish is something I just figured out. Every mistake is real. You get the actual process of learning — not polished expert content from someone who forgot what it felt like to be lost.

"The best way to learn is to teach. I'm learning RE in public so you don't have to start alone."

If you want to understand how software really works — how game protection systems operate, how malware hides, how binaries are structured — this is where to start. No background required.
